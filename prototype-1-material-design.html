Graph Neural Networks for Recommendation Systems
Authors: Zhang et al. Year: 2023 Venue: ICML Citations: 127
Abstract
This paper presents a novel approach to recommendation systems using graph neural networks. We introduce a multi-layer GNN architecture that captures both local and global user-item interactions, achieving state-of-the-art performance on multiple datasets. Our method addresses the cold-start problem through innovative embedding techniques and demonstrates significant improvements in recommendation accuracy.
Key Contributions
• Novel GNN architecture for recommendation systems
• Solution to cold-start problem
• State-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets
• Open-source implementation available
